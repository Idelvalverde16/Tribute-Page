Eden Hazard

The prince who never became king

Here's a time line of Eden Hazard's life:

  • 1991 - Date of birth: 7 January; Place of birth: La Louvière, Belgium.
  • 2005 - Hazard joined Lille and spent two years developing in the club's local sports school.
  • 2007 - Early Lille career.
  • 2008–2010 - Debut season in France and individual success.
  • 2010–2011 - League double, Ligue 1 Player of the Year and continued individual success.
  • 2011–2012 - Final season in France.
  • 2012–2013 - Debut season in England and first trophy.
  • 2013–2015 - Premier League winner and individual success.
  • 2018–2019 - Final season in England.
  • 2021–2023 - Continued struggle, departure, and retirement.
